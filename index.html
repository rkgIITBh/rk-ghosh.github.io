Prof. R. K. Ghosh (Personal Webpage)


Current affiliations:

  • Adjunct Professor BITS Mesra

Experience:

Strong education professional, skilled in Distributed Systems, Wireless Networking, Mobile Computing and Wireless Sensor networks. Have been a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kanpur and IIT Guwahati. Dr. Ghosh has worked on several projects related to parallel processing, distributed systems, mobile and cloud computing, some of which involved international collaborations with researchers from the University of Trento (Italy) Airbus Industrie (France), Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (CSIRO, Australia), University of Melbourne (Australia), University of Texas at Arlington (USA), and Hanyang University (Korea). He is an associate editor of Sadhana Indian Academy of Science Proceedings in Engineering Sciences. Dr. Ghosh has also been associated with organization of several peer reviewed conferences in the general parallel &s; distributed processing, wireless networking as General Chair, Program Chair and TPC member.
