R.K. Ghosh is currently a Visiting Professor in the Department EECS at Indian Institute of Technology Bhilai. Earlier he has served as a Professor in the Dept of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur and also held the position of a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. A few of the other positions he had held in the past include a UN Teacher Fellow at the International Institute for Software Technology (IIST) Macau, a Visiting Scientist at INRIA Sophia Antipolis, France, and a Visiting Faculty in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington, USA. His primary research interests are in Mobile Computing, Wireless Networking. He also has some interests in Cloud Computing and Data Analytics.
